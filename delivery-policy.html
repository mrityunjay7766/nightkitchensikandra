🚚 Delivery and Extra Charges Policy

We are committed to providing you with the best service possible, ensuring your order is fresh and delivered safely to your door. All our products are sold at their **MRP (Maximum Retail Price)**. However, to cover the operational costs associated with delivery, certain service charges apply in addition to the product MRP.

1. Components of Extra Charges

The extra fee added to your total bill is calculated to cover the following essential services:

Charge Name Description
Product Packaging Fee The cost of high-quality, secure packaging materials required to maintain the safety and freshness of your food items during transit.
Delivery Personnel Charge Compensation for our dedicated delivery partners who ensure your order reaches your specified address promptly.
Fuel/Transportation Charges The cost associated with fuel and vehicle maintenance necessary for the timely transportation of your order based on distance.

2. Delivery Area and Timing

  • Service Area: We only serve customers within **Sikandra** and specific adjacent zones. Please verify your address during checkout.
  • Operating Hours: Our service is available from **[3:00 PM] to [8:00 PM]**.
  • Delivery Estimate: Estimated delivery time is typically **[30 to 45] minutes**, but this can vary due to heavy traffic or unforeseen circumstances.

✅ Important Note: All extra charges are clearly itemized on your bill during the checkout process. Please review the total amount before making the payment.

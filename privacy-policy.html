🔒 Privacy Policy

Night Kitchen values your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order or interact with our website, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal Identification Information: Your name, email address, phone number, and precise delivery address. (This information is essential for fulfilling your order.)
  • Payment Information: We do not directly store credit card details. All financial transactions are processed securely through UPI or trusted payment gateways.
  • Usage Data: We may collect non-personal information such as your IP address, browser type, and time spent on our website to improve our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following primary purposes:

  • To process and successfully deliver your orders.
  • To communicate with you regarding your order status or any service issues.
  • To enhance and improve the functionality and offerings of our services.
  • To prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activity.

3. Sharing Your Information

  • We share your personal information (Name, Address, Phone Number) with our **Delivery Partners** to enable them to complete the delivery service.
  • We **do not sell, trade, or rent** your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Information may be disclosed if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access and misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data.

5. Changes to this Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any significant changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
